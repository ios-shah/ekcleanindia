Welcome To Ek Clean India Mission

A clean India would be the best tribute India could pay to Mahatma Gandhi on his 150 birth anniversary in 2019,” said Shri Narendra Modi as he launched the Ek Clean India Mission at Rajpath in New Delhi. On 2nd October 2014, Ek Clean India Mission was launched throughout length and breadth of the country as a national movement. The campaign aims to achieve the vision of a ‘Clean India’ by 2nd October 2019.

INTRODUCTION TO PROJECT..

Ek Clean India is the solution developed by TEAM-BETA during the hackathon which is an online platform that tries to ease the process of registering a complaint regarding cleanness in your locality, suggestions or feedback from anywhere in the country through emails and WhatsApp which are handled by bots. The complaints, suggestions, and feedbacks are collected and then forwarded to the respective authority so they can take the relevant action. One can also register themselves as a Volunteer to support their mission to Make India Cleaner.
